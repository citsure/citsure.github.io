Privacy policy

Last updated: June 2026

This policy explains what personal data CitSure collects, why, where it's kept, and the rights you have over it. We keep data collection to the absolute minimum.

Who we are

CitSure is an independent tool that provides free practice for the Life in the UK test. It is not affiliated with the Home Office or UK Government. For any privacy question, contact us at hello@citsure.co.uk.

What we collect

  • Your email address — but only if you choose to join our early-bird waitlist. If you don't join, we never ask for or store it.
  • Anonymous usage data — we use Cloudflare Web Analytics to count page views and measure how quickly pages load. It is privacy-first: it does not use cookies and does not collect information that identifies you personally.

We do not use advertising trackers, and we do not build profiles of visitors.

Why we collect it

If you join the waitlist, we use your email for one purpose: to let you know when CitSure's full question bank launches and to share early-bird access. Our lawful basis for this is your consent, which you give by submitting the form — and which you can withdraw at any time.

Where it's stored

Waitlist email addresses are collected and stored by Formspree, a third-party form service, on our behalf. Anonymous analytics are processed by Cloudflare. Both providers have their own privacy terms covering how they handle data.

How long we keep it

We keep your email only as long as needed for the purpose above — to tell you about the launch. If you ask us to remove you, or once the waitlist is no longer needed, we delete it.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law you have the right to ask us to:

  • tell you what data we hold about you,
  • correct it if it's wrong,
  • delete it, or
  • stop using it (you can withdraw consent and unsubscribe at any time).

To do any of these, email hello@citsure.co.uk. If you're unhappy with how we've handled your data, you can also complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO), the UK's data protection regulator, at ico.org.uk.

Sharing

We never sell your data, and we don't share it with anyone for marketing. The only parties that process it are the service providers named above, purely so the site and waitlist can work.

Changes to this policy

If we change how we handle data, we'll update this page and the date at the top.

CitSure is an independent practice tool. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the Home Office or UK Government.

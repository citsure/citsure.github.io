About CitSure

CitSure is an independent practice tool for the Life in the UK test, built around a single principle: get the facts right.

What CitSure is

We make free, timed mock tests built exactly like the real exam — 24 questions, 45 minutes, with a clear explanation for every answer. It's for anyone preparing for settlement or British citizenship who wants realistic practice they can actually trust.

Our accuracy promise

The Life in the UK test matters — people's settlement and citizenship applications depend on it. Yet many practice tools let learners down with out-of-date facts, broken scoring, and questions with no real answer. CitSure is built to be the opposite. Here's how we keep it right:

  • Every question is written in our own words from publicly available facts — never copied from the official handbook.
  • Each one is fact-checked and carries the date it was last reviewed.
  • We review the question bank on a regular schedule, and facts that can change over time — like the number of seats in a devolved parliament — are flagged and re-checked every cycle.
  • When something does slip through, we want to know, and we fix it quickly.

Honest by design

We're independent, and we say so plainly. We don't pretend to be official, we don't make "guaranteed pass" promises, and we don't dress the site up to look like a government service. Your practice should be straight with you — that's the entire point of CitSure.

Not affiliated with the government

CitSure is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the Home Office or the UK Government. The official Life in the UK test is booked only through GOV.UK. We help you prepare for it — we don't provide the test itself or any qualification.

Spotted a mistake?

If you ever find an error in a question or an explanation, please tell us — accuracy is the whole reason CitSure exists, and a correction from you helps everyone who takes the test after you. Email hello@citsure.co.uk.
